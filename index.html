The Source

Pear Tree Well is Sussex's only natural mineral water bottled at source. Our 120ft deep borehole draws pure water from ancient rock in the protected High Weald countryside.

In a world of polluted water, our spring remains pristine—naturally filtered through layers of rock, rich in 16+ essential minerals with a perfectly balanced pH.
