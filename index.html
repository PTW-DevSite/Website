The Source

Pear Tree Well is Sussex's only natural mineral water bottled at source. Our 120ft deep borehole draws pure water from ancient rock beneath the protected High Weald Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.

Naturally filtered over centuries, it emerges pristine and rich in essential minerals with a perfectly balanced pH.
